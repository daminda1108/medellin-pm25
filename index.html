Model options & honest framing for the curious

This site exists to prove a method built for cities WITHOUT monitors (like Kandy, Sri Lanka): the maps are built as if Medellín had almost no ground data — a satellite level anchor and physics for the street-scale pattern — and then scored against the real (withheld) monitoring network. Medellín's pollution is mostly locally emitted; Kandy's arrives episodically from across the sea. The demonstration proves the machinery and the value of monitors, not that every regime is equally predictable. Map hours follow the driver archive (2021–2022 partially covered).
